Demonstration Stand · UPB

Electromagnetism teaching rig

A bench-top stand built to demonstrate electromagnetic induction in a hands-on, visual way. Mechanical frame designed in Fusion 360, FDM-printed and assembled with off-the-shelf coils and a permanent magnet. Used for mechanics & physics teaching at Politehnica University of Bucharest.

Demonstration Stands · UPB

Differential-induction vibration rigs

Two teaching stands built to demonstrate how differentially-induced vibration can loosen a bolted joint over time. One uses a toothed-gear cam to generate the excitation, the other uses an eccentric-mass motor — the comparison is the experiment. Built for vibration & mechanical-engineering coursework at UPB.
